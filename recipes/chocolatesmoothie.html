Chocolate Smoothie

Description

This chocolate smoothie is my go-to whenever I'm craving something sweet! It has a rich, chocolately flavor with satiating ingredients that will keep you full.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 frozen avocado (in chunks)
  • 1/2 frozen banana avocado
  • 1.5 cups almond milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen dark chocolate chips or pieces
  • 1 tablespoon dark chocolate powder
  • 2 dates

Steps

  1. Place frozen avocado, banana, almond milk, and dates in the blender
  2. Blend on pulse. If the smoothie is too thick and not blending properly, add more almond milk
  3. Once smoothie is smooth, add in the dark chocolate chips / pieces and powder. Blend again until smooth
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
