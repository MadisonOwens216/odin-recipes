Peanut Butter and Banana Smoothie

Description

This simple smoothie is so easy to make, and uses ingredients that you most lilkely already have in your fridge / pantry! While I personally enjoy this smoothie as a post-workout pick-me-up, this would also be an awesome breakfast option or mid-day treat. If you want to create a more complex flavor profile, consider adding in some dark chocolate or coffee to take this smoothie to the next level.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 frozen banana (chopped)
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1.5 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Steps

  1. Place chopped frozen bananas, milk, peanut butter and vanilla into the blender
  2. Blend on pulse. If the smoothie is too thick and not blending properly, add more almond milk
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
